# Lesson 1.2: Propositional Equivalences

---

## EX 1.2.1
**ResponseType**: Multiple Choice  
**Difficulty**: 1  
**ExerciseType**:   
**Question**: Which law states that P ∨ ¬P is always true?
**Options**:  
- A. Law of Contradiction  
- B. Law of Excluded Middle  
- C. Law of Identity  
- D. Law of Double Negation  
**Solution**: B

---

## EX 1.2.2

**Difficulty**: 1  
**ExerciseType**:

**Options**:

- B. ¬P ∧ Q  
- C. P ∨ Q  
- D. P ∧ ¬Q  
**Solution**: A

**ExerciseType**:

- C. P ∧ Q ≡ Q ∧ P  
- D. ¬P ∨ Q ≡ Q ∨ ¬P
