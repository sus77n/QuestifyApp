## EX 1.2.26  
**Type**: Short Answer  
**Question**: Let P, Q, R be propositions:  
P: The device is powered on.  
Q: The device is connected to Wi-Fi.  
R: The device can access the internet.  
Express the statement "If the device is powered on and connected to Wi-Fi, then it can access the internet" as a logical expression.  
**Solution**: (P ∧ Q) → R

---

## EX 1.2.27  
**Type**: Short Answer  
**Question**: Let P, Q, R be propositions:  
P: The student submits the assignment.  
Q: The student attends the review session.  
R: The student passes the course.  
The statement "If the student does not submit the assignment or attends the review session, then the student passes the course" corresponds to which logical statement?  
**Solution**: (¬P ∨ Q) → R
